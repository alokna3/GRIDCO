Background of RE Nodal Agency

Odisha RE Policy, 2022 was notified on 30.11.2022 and released during the Make in Odisha Conclave, 2022. The policy envisions renewable energy capacity addition of ~10+ GW in the state by 2030. The policy focuses on enabling “Just Transition” in the state and focuses on developing a pipeline of RE projects so that the energy needs of the state can be met from sources within the state in a sustainable manner. GRIDCO has been nominated as the Nodal Agency vide Notification No 12284 dated 15.12.2022 The Single Window Clearance Committee has also been constituted
