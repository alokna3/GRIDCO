Role of RE Nodal Agency

Act as secretariat to Single Window Committee (SWC) Act as a single window facilitator for allotment of projects/clearance of proposals received from the Private Sector/CPSUs/SPSUs. Identification of projects to be awarded through competitive bidding. Prepare, maintain, and continuously update a registry of projects across all technologies. Perform resource potential assessment of all technologies. Act as a facilitator to obtain all statutory clearances. Resolution of Policy level issues for accelerating the deployment of various RE technologies in the State. Coordinate with different stakeholders to facilitate the implementation of the Policy. Coordinate with support the OERC in framing various Regulations and orders that are required for the implementation of the Policy. Frame the Rules and detailed procedure for implementation of the Policy and submit to DoE for finalization.
